About FermTrend

Standalone browser app for Brewer's Friend SG-stability analysis. Pure-JS port of the FG analyzer and SG-trend chart that live in the FermVault Brain dashboard, so results are byte-for-byte identical.

Source: github.com/keglevelmonitor/fermtrend. MIT-licensed. Deploy your own copy in 15 minutes -- see the README.

Your API key never leaves your browser except to reach the Cloudflare Worker CORS proxy, which forwards it verbatim to Brewer's Friend and does not log or persist it.
